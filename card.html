Lion

Females usually stay in their mothers' prides for life, unless food scarcity forces them out.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Length: 8-9 feet
  • Average Lifespan:: 10-14 years
  • Habitat: Africa

Lions coats are yellow-gold, and adult males have shaggy manes that range in color from blond to reddish-brown to black.
